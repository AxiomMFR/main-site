Our Core Services

Axiom fills a critical operational gap by executing complex, custom machining projects alongside robust, heavy mechanical maintenance. Every project follows a strict, documented quality system aligned with ISO 9001 principles.

  • CNC & Manual Machining
    Precision milling and turning for low-volume production, specialized fixtures, and tight-tolerance component replacement. We specialize in reverse-engineering and digitizing heavily worn or entirely obsolete original parts, providing an exact mechanical fit even when manufacturer documentation no longer exists.
  • Welding & Fabrication
    Custom structural component fabrication, field welding modifications, and heavy machinery attachment repair designed to survive severe load and environment conditions.
  • On-Site Field Service & Heavy Equipment Repair
    We bring the shop to your site. Axiom specializes in scheduled field service and heavy mechanical repairs, providing dedicated project execution designed to get your machinery back online efficiently. Repair capabilities cover compact, utility, agricultural, and heavy fleet assets from skid steers to major dirt and farm iron.
  • Compact & Utility Equipment Repair
    Rigorous diagnostic troubleshooting, regular fleet servicing, and complete mechanical component repairs for utility assets, skid steers, mini excavators, and utility tractors.
  • Powersports Repair
    Top-end engine rebuilding, precision drivetrain tuning, and mechanical service for commercial, utility, and recreational ATVs, UTVs, dirt bikes, and personal watercraft.
  • Portable Line Boring
    Field-deployed bore reclamation, machining worn articulated joints, pin bores, and pivot points back to true factory dimensional tolerances on site.
  • Engine Repair & Rebuild
    Engine diagnostics, cylinder head servicing, and complete long-block rebuilds on industrial gas and heavy utility diesel powerplants.
  • Hydraulic Repair
    Systematic teardown, inspection, re-sealing, and servicing of hydraulic cylinders, pumps, motors, valves, and power units.
